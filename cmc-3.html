About CMC 3

CMC 3 stands tall in the heart of the Financial District, offering a unique blend of modern business environments and luxurious amenities. Spread over more than 48,000 m², CMC 3 is crafted to empower professionals and families with flexible spaces and an exceptional lifestyle.

Experience smart layouts, high-end offices, and advanced facilities—all designed to foster productivity, comfort, and growth in Egypt’s most dynamic new city.

Architectural Excellence

The architecture of CMC 3 reflects a harmony of bold lines and modern glass facades, maximizing natural daylight and panoramic city views. Inspired by Egypt’s natural beauty, CMC 3 is built for sustainability and elegance at every corner.

Energy-efficient features and advanced insulation guarantee a green, comfortable, and future-ready environment for all occupants.

Amenities

CMC 3 offers a curated range of amenities and services to support a thriving, modern community, including:

  • Executive Sky Lounge
  • Business Center
  • Fitness Club
  • Spacious Meeting Rooms
  • Conference Facilities
  • Café & Retail Area
  • Secured Underground Parking
