About CMC 2

Located in the vibrant Financial District of the New Administrative Capital, CMC 2 is designed to redefine the business and lifestyle experience. With a total area exceeding 50,000 m², CMC 2 stands as a state-of-the-art destination for ambitious entrepreneurs and modern families.

The tower features smart layouts, spacious offices, and elegant administrative units, all supported by advanced facilities and seamless connectivity, offering the perfect environment for growth and success.

Architectural Innovation

CMC 2 showcases unique design elements with flowing lines and striking glass facades for panoramic views and natural lighting. The building’s structure is inspired by the harmony and strength of nature, ensuring both beauty and durability.

Modern insulation and energy-efficient technologies make CMC 2 a smart choice for a sustainable business and a comfortable working environment.

Amenities

CMC 2 provides a comprehensive set of amenities and services for a dynamic and productive environment, including:

  • Sky Lounge
  • Break Area
  • Waiting Area
  • Modern Gym
  • Meeting Rooms
  • Conference Hall
  • Spacious Underground Parking
